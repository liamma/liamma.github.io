Notes for COMP767-RL

COMP767 RL Lec 1

Multi-Bandit

Definintion and Notation

Choose an action $A_t$ from $k$ possibilities, and receive a real-valued reward $R_t$

  • The reward depends onlu on the action taken;
  • It is i.i.d distributed

Denote the true value:
$$
q_(a)=\mathbb{E}[R_t|A_t=a], \forall a \in{1,\dots,k}
$$
Denote The estimates of action-value at time-step $t$:
$$
Q_t(a)\approx q_(a)
$$
The greedy action at time $t$ :
$$
A^*_t=\arg\max_a Q_t(a)
$$

  • exploiting : $A_t=A^*_t$
  • exploring: $A_t\neq A^*_t$

Need to balance exploiting and exploring

Action-Value Method

  • Learns the action-value estimates

Example:

estimate action values as sample average:
$$
Q_t(a)=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{t-1}R_i\cdot \mathbf{1}_{A_i=a}}{\sum_{i=1}^{t-1}\mathbf{1}_{A_i=a}}
$$

$$
\lim_{N_t(a)\to \infty}Q_t(a)=q_*(a)
$$
